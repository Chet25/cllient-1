Who am I?

I am an innovator and adventurer. I believe that change is the only constant we have in the world. A person who is capable of change will always succeed no matter the circumstances, “Be water” some may call it. Our technological world changes everyday hence, it is even more important for a technical innovator to be capable of change.

What have I done?

I have explored about every field of the technical industry from cutting edge AI to decades old physical computing. I have experienced the failures and successes of many projects and a wide variety of challenges which comes with them. I have made games, websites, apps, algorithms, ML models, APIs, databases, scrapers, LED controllers and contributed to open source.

What can I do?

I can do anything that I can set my mind to. I can learn any new technology, framework, procedure or tool that would help to make a project come to life. I am capable of basically anything to support a project in any way.
